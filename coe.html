Code of Ethics

  1. Our Promise to Heal: We’re here for everyone in our city, friend or enemy, helping without judging. We treat everyone with respect and care, no matter who they are.
  2. Always Professional: We take pride in our work and stay professional, even when things get crazy.We act with integrity, setting the example for excellent medical care.
  3. Keeping Secrets: We keep what we hear about patients private. Their stories are safe with us. We only talk about patient details with those who need to know.
  4. Stronger Together: We work best when we work together. Our teamwork is key to helping everyone who needs us. We make sure no call for help is ignored.
  5. Always Learning: We’re always learning to be ready for anything our city throws at us. By sharing what we learn, we all become better at helping others
  6. Safety First: We stick to our safety rules to protect everyone who comes to us for help. We’re always trying to improve how we care for our patients.
  7. Fair and Unbiased: We stick to the rules and make decisions based on what’s best for everyone, free from outside pressure or bias. We treat everyone equally and offer the same level of care to all.
  8. Solving Problems: We handle conflicts calmly and with authority. We focus on the health and wellbeing of our patients, leaving personal issues aside.
  9. Always on Call: Our duty to help doesn’t stop when we’re off duty. When the city calls, we’re there. We’re ready to save lives, in the hospital or on the streets, living the values of our hospital every day.
